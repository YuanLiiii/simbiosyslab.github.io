SimBioSys

The Emory Lab for Computational Systems and their Biologial Connections (SimBioSys) is a partnership of researchers exploring the management and dissemination of information. Simbiosys lab is run by Drs. Avani Wildani and Ymir Vigfusson, both of whom are Assistant Professors in Math and Computer Science. We are rapidly growing lab and currently support 1 postdoc, 5 PhD students, 2 MS students, and 4 dedicated undergraduates as well as several visitors and rotation students. Our current research areas include such wide ranging topics as predictive caching, storage system analysis, computer security, wireless mesh network optimization, biologically plausible neural networks, theory of learning, and disease transmission network models.
